Our pre-trade and post-trade solutions offering helps market participants control counterparty and regulatory risks through a range of tools that manage balance-sheet exposure, compression and optimisation services.

Every day thousands of orders and committed quotes for OTC derivatives are received from the largest market participants across TP, ICAP and PVM's 3,000\+ platforms, trading venues and liquidity pools in 86 locations across 27 countries. Trade and Order provides those market participants with a curated, normalised and standardised feed of all the orders and resulting transactions, with full taxonomy, timestamps and audit trail.

## Trade and Order for IPV – Use Cases

### Back-testing

Checking independent price data against real market transactions to corroborate that the independent data really does represent active market levels.

### 'Levelling' in the Fair Value Hierarchy

Obtaining real market transactions and quotes to evidence market activity and liquidity in specific assets to allocate to levels 1, 2 and 3 within the Fair Value Hierarchy (IFRS 13/ASC TOPIC 820).

### Prudent Valuation

Close-out costs AVA – sourcing market bid-offer levels to construct a confidence interval in which an exit price would be when exiting a position.

Concentration AVA – sourcing transaction volumes/notionals to construct a confidence interval for liquidity when exiting a position.
