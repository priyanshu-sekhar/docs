## WebSocket

**Parameta’s** WebSocket service provides an efficient, scalable solution for real-time market data streaming. Using WebSocket technology, our service establishes a persistent, connection between the client and our servers, ensuring that market data such as quotes, trades, and price updates are delivered instantly and continuously with minimal latency.

WebSocket delivery is ideal for clients that require immediacy of real-time data via JSON or into standard desktop applications without interrupting their workflow.

The WebSocket JSON output can be readily integrated into trading platforms, providing clients with the tools they need to monitor and respond to market movements with pace.

### Key Features

- **Real-time Streaming:** Continuous updates of market data price changes (including trades and quotes) optimised for fast data transmission.
- **Efficiency:** Persistent connection reduces network overhead and unnecessary resource consumption. Data packets are optimised for continuous streaming.
- **Scalability:** Capable of handling numerous simultaneous client connections, making it suitable for high-traffic environments.
- **Secure and Reliable:** Parameta data transmitted over WebSocket is secured using the SHA-2 family of cryptographic hashing (AES – Advanced Encryption Standard).
- **Secure Management:** Web-based admin tool supports HTTPS for secure remote management.

## Marketfeed API

**Parameta Solutions** deliver data via an API that leverages Marketfeed protocol designed to deliver OTC market pricing in a reliable and auditable form to all clients. The feed is delivered as a broadcast data stream over TCP/IP connections.

It is derived from LSEG’s Marketfeed protocol utilising Reuter’s Appendix A as it’s field data library. This feed can be delivered via internet or leased line.

The feed caters for delivery of data structures in Marketfeed-format records, chains and pages. Data is categorised by **Parameta** at the “stub” level (first 5 characters of a record) in keeping with our proprietary taxonomy rules.

## EMD Direct Connect (Enterprise Market Data Distribution)

For customers looking to take market data directly into their infrastructure, **Parameta's** EMD Direct Connect service offers a seamless and scalable solution for streaming real-time data into their data network. We offer a multitude of connection options to tailor to the client’s specific technical setup, locale and data distribution requirements.

### Key Features

- **Full Access to Inventory:** Clients of EMD Direct Connect can access Parameta’s full realtime SURFIX inventory and customisation options.
- **Reduced Complexity and Streamlined Operations:** Direct connectivity simplifies the data flow, reducing the need for multiple intermediaries and thereby streamlining operations.
- **Enhanced Performance and Reliability:** EMD Direct Connect will ensure high-speed data transfer with minimal latency (millisecond precision if required), leading to improved performance and reliability of data services.
- **Cost Efficiency:** By minimizing the number of intermediaries and optimising data flow, EMD Direct Connect can lead to significant cost savings in data management and distribution.
- **Direct Billing and Data Permissioning:** Clients can come direct to Parameta to browse/source new data, define and distribute that data via their permissioning system and manage their billing costs in an efficient and streamlined manner.

### Connectivity for EMD

**Parameta’s** EMD Direct Connect platform resides in AWS cloud. Our services are currently available globally across **EU-WEST-1, US-EAST-1, and AP-SOUTHEAST-1**.

As the service runs in the AWS cloud, our recommended approach is to utilise AWS **PrivateLink** within the same region (e.g., EU-WEST-1, see **Example Configurations**) for optimum setup.
