title: "Historical Data"
---

**Parameta Solutions** offers comprehensive market data across all asset classes in the OTC space going back several decades in many cases.

Our services offer access to **Parameta’s** industry leading inventory of time-series data through secure and reliable methods such as:

| Delivery Method | Supported Formats |
| --- | --- |
| **SFTP** | CSV, JSON, XML, XLSX, Parquet, TXT |
| **Snowflake** | Standard format |
| **Amazon S3** | All standard formats |
| **Google Cloud Storage** | All standard formats |
| **Azure Blob** | All standard formats |

This service allows clients to easily obtain vast bespoke datasets (years of tick-by-tick data, end-of-day prices or specific historical quotes) that can drive deeper insights and enhance trading models.
