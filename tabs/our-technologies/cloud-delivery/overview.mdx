## Snowflake

**Parameta Solution's** data is stored in an industry-leading enterprise cloud data warehouse enabling seamless data access to any access service, application, or cloud-based infrastructure.

**Snowflake** houses our full inventory of pre-trade, post-trade, indicative/quote and historic data (going back to 2006). Data is available in proprietary Commercial Packages that are split by asset class and currency.

## Cloud Repository Delivery

For clients preferring to receive data in their own cloud environment, Parameta can deliver snap files and time series data directly to:

- **Snowflake Reader or Direct shares** — Delivered via User Defined Functions (UDFs) to your Snowflake account
- **Amazon S3** — Delivery to your S3 buckets with configurable IAM permissions
- **Google Cloud Storage** — Delivery to GCS with service account authentication
- **Azure Blob Storage** — Delivery to Azure containers with SAS token or managed identity
