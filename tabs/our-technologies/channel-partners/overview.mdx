## Available Data

Through our channel partners, you can access:

- **Real-time Streaming** — Live tick-by-tick OTC market data
- **Delayed Feeds** — Time-delayed market data for compliance use cases
- **End-of-Day** — Daily snapshots and closing prices
- **Historical Data** — Time series data for backtesting and analytics

## Getting Started

1. **Contact Your Partner** — Reach out to your existing channel partner representative
2. **Request Parameta Data** — Specify the Parameta data products you need
3. **Configure Access** — Work with your partner to enable the data feed
4. **Start Consuming** — Access data through your partner's delivery technology
