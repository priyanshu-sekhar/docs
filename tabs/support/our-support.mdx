## Contact Us

For all support enquiries, reach us at:

Email: [support@parametasolutions.com](mailto:support@parametasolutions.com)

## What to include in your request

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please provide:

- Your client or account name
- The product or service affected (e.g. WebSocket, Snowflake, EOD files)
- A clear description of the issue, including any error messages
- **Timestamps** of when the issue occurred (with timezone)
- Any relevant sample data, record names, SQL code or screenshots

## Response times

Our team monitors support requests during business hours across our global offices.
