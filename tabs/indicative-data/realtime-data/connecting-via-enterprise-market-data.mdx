## Connectivity Solutions

We offer various connectivity solutions for EMD. As this service runs in the AWS cloud, our recommended approach is to utilize **AWS PrivateLink** within the same region (e.g., **EU-WEST-1**). Other options are available; cross-region PrivateLink connectivity, clients can use a leased line (B2B) or an internet-based solution.

## Configuration and Protocol Recommendations

For **EMD-to-EMD** configurations, we recommend that clients configure their respective EMD host services as sink-driven and set the protocol to datatype 6 to ensure efficient data delivery. The client’s EMD host will initiate the connection to the Parameta Solutions EMD system, with data flow being pulled by the client.
