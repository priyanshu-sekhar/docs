## Examples Commercial Packages for Interest Rates

<Tip>
  The name of the commercial packages are used to name objects provided to clients in our data warehouse.
</Tip>

Commercial packages are grouped by a parent (_e.g. ICAP Interest Rates_), with the commercial package itself being _ICAP EUR IRO Premium_ :

### ICAP Interest Rates

- **ICAP EUR IRO Premium** - EUR interest rate options including swaptions, caps, floors, and CMS spread products. Spot and forward start coverage.
- **ICAP SwapPX** - CAD and USD interest rate swaps, OIS, basis swaps, and swap spreads with focus on North American markets.
- **ICAP IR Swaps (G7)** - G7 currency swaps covering vanilla IRS, OIS, FRAs, basis swaps, and swap spreads across major developed markets.
- **ICAP Interest Rate Derivatives** - Comprehensive derivatives package with swaps, options, FRAs, and basis swaps across 30 currencies including emerging markets.
- **ICAP IR Swaps (Global)** - Global swap coverage across 39 currencies including vanilla IRS, OIS, cross-currency swaps, and non-deliverable products.
- **ICAP Interest Rate Options** - Global options across 16 currencies covering swaptions, caps, floors, and CMS products.
- **ICAP IR Options (ATM G7)** - At-the-money swaptions, caps, and floors for G7 currencies (AUD, CAD, CHF, EUR, GBP, JPY, USD).
- **ICAP IR Options (Global)** - Comprehensive global options package with ATM and OTM structures across 20 currencies including emerging markets.

### Totan

- **Totan Interest Rate Option** - JPY interest rate options including swaptions, caps, and floors.
- **Totan Interest Rate Derivatives** - Multi-currency swaps, OIS, and FRAs covering EUR, JPY, and USD markets.

### Tullet Prebon Interest Rates

- **TP Rates** - Global rates package across 37 currencies covering swaps, OIS, FRAs, basis swaps, and sovereign bonds.
- **TP Interest Rate Option Volatility** - Comprehensive volatility surfaces across 17 currencies with swaptions, caps, floors, and structured options.
- **TP IR Options (ATM G7)** - G7 at-the-money swaptions and caps/floors with collar and strangle structures.
- **TP IR Swaps (G7)** - G7 vanilla swaps, OIS, basis swaps, and cross-currency products.
- **TP IR Swaps (Global)** - Global swap coverage across 38 currencies including non-deliverable and inflation-adjusted products.
- **TP IR Options (Global)** - Global options across 27 currencies with ATM/OTM structures, CMS products, and exotic strategies.

### TP Sitico Interest Rates

- **Sitico Interest Rate Derivatives** - CNY-denominated swaps including cross-currency products and OIS.
