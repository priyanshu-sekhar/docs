---
title: "Record Naming Convention"
description: "This page provides the technical specifications for Parameta Solution's multi-brand naming conventions. Each one of our multi-brand records follow the naming convention below."
---

```json
<RECORD_NAME>:<LOCATIONS>.<CLEARING_TYPES>.<PRICE_TYPES>.<PRICING_FREQUENCIES>!<COMPANIES>
```

<Note>
  Example `[[RF][OIS]][EUR][01Y][AOAEST]:{LDN}.{LCH}.{QTE}.{RTM}!{TP}`
</Note>

| # | Component | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **STUB** | First 5 characters — Asset Class and Instrument/Product (e.g., `RF` = Risk Free Rates, `OIS` = Overnight Index Swap) |
| 2 | **CCY** | Next 3 or 6 characters — Currency(s). Two CCYs are possible (e.g., Cross Currency Swap or Cross Currency Basis Swap) |
| 3 | **Tenor** | Trade tenor. May include one, two or three tenors after the CCY (e.g., `01Y01Y` for a Fwd Starting market, `02Y05Y07Y` for a Butterfly) |
| 4 | **Daycount/Basis** | Only available in certain markets. `AOA` = Act/360 Ann, `EST` = ESTR discounting |
| 5 | **Location** | Region the prices are coming from. `GBL` = 'Follow the Sun' record contributed from more than one region |
| 6 | **Clearing** | Clearing House used for trading (e.g., `LCH` = London Clearing House) |
| 7 | **Price Type** | Type of prices on the instrument (e.g., `QTE` = Quote) |
| 8 | **Frequency** | How often the record updates (e.g., `RTM` = Realtime) |
| 9 | **Company** | Brand the prices are coming from (e.g., `TP` = Tullett Prebon, `IC` = ICAP) |
