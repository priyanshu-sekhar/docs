## 1. Data Addition Notifications

When data is added to the feed a Data Addition Notification (DAN) is sent out. See example format below:

| Record Type | Brand | Record | Record Description | Stub | Additional Information |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Multibrand | ICAP | IVSWOUSDATM01W01Y:NYK.BIL.QTE.RTM!IC | Swaption Straddle ATM USD 1 Week option vs 1 Year SOFR | IVSWO | The record in Column C will be added in the upcoming weeks. Please email [support@parametasolutions.com](mailto:support@parametasolutions.com) to inquire about any additional data |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

## 2. Data Change Notifications

When data is changed on the feed a Data Change Notification (DCN) is sent out. See example format below:

| Record Type | Brand | Record | Record Description | Stub | Additional Information | Effective Date |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Multibrand | ICAP | IREFPAUD18M3FUOIS:SYD.LCH.QTE.RTM!IC | Exchange of Futures for Physical AUD 3y Bond Futures/OIS 18 Month | IREFP | Fid 134 will now show the realtime MID instead of Close MID | 16-Jun-26 |
| ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |
